About

Alan Stoffer

Alan began his career in consulting and received an MBA from UW Madison. He worked at General Mills where he learned the fundamentals of Marketing from one of the most esteemed companies in the industry. He then moved to Silicon Valley where he spent years building marketing functions from scratch inside Intuit, Google's Waymo project (first product marketing hire) and Meta's News Feed team. These organizations set the bar for strategic thinking and data-driven execution extraordinarily high.

After leaving big tech, Alan co-founded Worqflow Marketing, where he built a team of 12 and ran digital marketing programs for companies in many industries including manufacturing, SaaS, and ecommerce. Today he directs the Marketing Leadership Institute at UW Madison's Wisconsin School of Business, where he connects MBA teams with Fortune 500 companies on real marketing challenges and teaches an AI marketing course that has been attended by over 250 working professionals. He keeps a small consulting practice for the engagements where he can make a difference.

  • MBA, Wisconsin School of Business, UW Madison
  • First Product Marketing hire, Google (Waymo)
  • Product Marketing Lead, Meta (Facebook News Feed)
  • Co-Founder, Worqflow Marketing Agency
  • Director, Marketing Leadership Institute, UW Madison
